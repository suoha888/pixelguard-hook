PixelGuard is a next-generation Uniswap v4 Hook that intercepts large dumps, charges a penalty fee to fund a contract treasury, and mints yield-bearing on-chain collectible NFT receipts to traders.

01

Swap & Intercept

Traders trade on the PixelGuard pool. If a swap size is large (>= 5 tokens), it triggers a penalty LP fee (1.00%) and a **0.50% Hook Fee** to protect liquidity.

02

Yield Accumulation

The 0.50% Hook Fee is directed into the contract's treasury. An automated staking accumulator (`rewardPerShare`) calculates dividends for all receipt holders.

03

Claim & Discount

NFT receipt holders claim accrued yield at any time. Holding the NFT also triggers an LP fee discount to **0.20%** (normal) and **0.80%** (guarded) for future swaps.
